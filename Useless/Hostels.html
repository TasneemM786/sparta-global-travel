Basics to Travelling

  • Use incognito mode when searching for flights.

    This is so that you don't get overcharged when you're looking for sites as most travel sites share cookies on what prices you've already been quoted.

  • Stay in Hostels

    It cannot be overstated how much cheaper hostels are to staying in hotels.

  • Make use of free services

    Save your money, use what's available to you - like free food.

  • Venture off the beaten path

    Tourist traps are a nightmare, go look around for good deals.

  • Treasure experiences over material things


    Souvenirs are overrated.
